## Executive Summary

**FlowAI** is a B2B SaaS platform that uses generative AI to automate repetitive workflows for small and mid‑sized teams (5–200 employees). It connects to existing tools (Slack, Gmail, Notion, Jira) and lets users create “AI agents” without code.

### At a Glance
- **Customer:** SMBs with 5–200 employees (ops, sales, product teams)  
- **MVP:** No‑code AI agent builder + 10 pre‑built workflow templates  
- **Value Prop:** Save 10+ hours/team/week with zero engineering  
- **Pricing:** $49/seat/month (annual) or $59/seat/month (monthly)  
- **Revenue Model:** 100% recurring subscription (SaaS)  
- **GTM:** Product‑led growth + outbound SDRs + partner ecosystem  

### Key Year‑1 Metrics

| ARR | $1.2M |
| Customers | 85 |

🟢 **Verdict:** FlowAI is **investable** – strong unit economics, clear customer pain, and a distribution‑first GTM plan. The biggest risk (AI model cost scaling) is hedged by tiered pricing and usage caps.
